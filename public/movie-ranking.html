The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

  1. In Time

    American science fiction action film where inhabitants of a society that uses time from one's lifespan as its primary currency, with each individual possessing a clock on their arm that counts down how long they have to live.

  2. About Time

    Romantic science fiction comedy-drama film. The film is about a young man with the ability to time travel who tries to change his past in hopes of improving his future.

  3. Big Mouth

    Thriller about an unsuccessful lawyer who gets involved in a murder case and is somehow fingered as genius swindler Big Mouse.
